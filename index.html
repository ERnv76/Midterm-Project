About Me

 I’m Vern Andre A. Manayan, 22 years old, born in November 2001. I am a BSIT student at Silliman University. There are six members in my family, and I have three siblings. I am the third child. I am originally from Oroquieta City, but due to my father’s work, we have moved from place to place. We lived in Pagadian City, then Cagayan de Oro, and finally Dumaguete City, where we have been living for 16 years. I studied at St. Paul University from grade school to junior high school, and finished my senior high school at Foundation Preparatory Academy.


In my free time, I love to play computer games, mostly first-person shooters, strategy games, and MMORPGs. This is one of the reasons why I chose BSIT because of my interest in computer games. I also enjoy listening to music when I’m not playing video games, especially neo-metal, classic rock, and punk pop.
